VitalSync: The AI-Powered Vernacular  
Guardian
Proposed Solution
A low-cost (<₹4k), screenless wearable that
monitors critical vitals (SpO2, HR, Temp) and
uses Edge AI to detect cardiac anomalies and
falls. It connects to a Multilingual Mobile App
designed for rural users, breaking literacy
barriers with voice-guided alerts in local
dialects.How it Solves the Problem:
Solves Language Barriers: Multilingual Voice UI
ensures illiterate or semi-literate rural patients
can understand medical alerts without reading.
Solves Cost: Replaces ₹40k+ smartbands with a
sub-₹4k focused medical device.
Solves False Alarms: Sensor Fusion confirm s
falls before calling for help.
Detailed Breakdown
Wearable Node: ESP32-C3 wristband with
"Touch-to-Measure" Copper Tape ECG and
continuous PPG.
Edge Intelligence: TinyML algorithms filter
noise and detect Arrhythmia/Falls locally.
Inclusive App Interface: A "Pictorial & Voice-
First" dashboard that speaks alerts (e.g., "Please
rest, heart rate high") in the patient's native
regional language.Key Innovations
"Cuffless" BP Estimation: Novel PTT algorithm
to estimate blood pressure trends.
Vernacular Voice Engine: Dynamic Text-to-
Speech (TTS) for health coaching in 10+ regional
languages.
Copper Tape Electrodes: Low-cost fabrication
for medical-grade signals

TECHNICAL APPROACH
                     1: Signal Acquisition & R eal-Time DSP
Precision Sampling: Utilizes a hardware timer interrupt
to sample ECG data at a strict 500Hz frequency via a
custom copper-tape interface, ensuring clinical-grade
accuracy for HRV analysis.
On-Chip Filtering: Implements a two-stage DSP
pipeline (50Hz Notch + 20Hz Butterworth) to eliminate
power-line hum and muscle noise artifacts before the
signal reaches the AI engine
2: Edge AI & Sensor Fusion Pipeline
TinyML Inference: Deploys a quantized 1D-CNN
model (Edge Impulse) directly on the ESP32-C3
(latency <20ms) to detect Arrhythmia patterns locally
without internet dependency.
Multi-Modal Logic: Uses Sensor Fusion to confirm
"Fall Events" by cross-referencing high-G impact
(Accelerometer) with sudden Heart Rate spikes, while
simultaneously calculating Cuffless BP via Pulse
Transit Time (PTT).
4: Cloud Analystics & Vernacular UX
Longitudinal Reports: Cloud backend aggregates data
to generate "Pre vs. Post Prescription" efficacy scores,
helping doctors quantify patient improvement over
weeks.
Offline Voice Engine: The mobile app pre-caches
audio assets to deliver Vernacular Voice Alerts (e.g., in
Marathi/Hindi) based on error codes, ensuring
accessibility even without active data.
3: Resilient IoT Architecture (Store-and-Forward)
Offline Resilience: buffers data to SPIFFS (Flash
Memory) during network outages and automatically
"flushes" historical logs to the MQTT broker upon
reconnection (QoS 1).
Power Optimization: Operates in Deep Sleep (<10µA),
waking only for scheduled vitals checks or critical
interrupt triggers (like a fall), extending battery life for
rural usage.
